The purpose of a web browser is to take the information given to it by a web page, and to render it for you to view. Due to how different companies and browsers want content to be viewed, some browsers can render things differently to others. At best, this could mean some minor graphical glitches or none at all. At worst, this could cause a loss of functionality.
